Section Title: Lord mayors (since 1919)
Table Section Text: The Official styled title of the Lord Mayor of Adelaide is The Right Honourable Lord Mayor of Adelaide. The styled title The Right Honourable, (which has no connection with the Privy Council) attaches to the title of Lord Mayor, and not to their names, and is relinquished upon leaving office.
