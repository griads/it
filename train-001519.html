List of YuruYuri episodes


Section Title: YuruYuri San☆Hai! (2015)
Table Section Text: None
No. Title Release date
1 "It is The Beginning of Amusement" Transcription: "Sore wa, Sunawachi, Goraku no Hajimaji" (Japanese: それは、すなわち、娯楽の始まり) October 5, 2015
The Amusement Club play the King Game, where Kyōko gives out ludicrous commands while Chinatsu hopes to become King in order to give Yui a command.
2 "Tremble in Fear" Transcription: "Saa, Obierugaii" (Japanese: さぁおびえるがいい) October 12, 2015
Yui asks the other girls to help get rid of a spider in her apartment before they all make some pancakes together, while Himawari and Sakurako misunderstand what the other is worrying about.
3 "No Self-Awareness" Transcription: "Jikaku nashi" (Japanese: 自覚なし) October 19, 2015
The first years do paintings of each of other for art class, with Chinatsu doing a terrifying representation of Akari while Himawari and Sakurako end up doing drawings that make fun of the other.
4 "The Night Will Connect Their Thoughts." Transcription: "Sono Yoru wa, Minna no Omoi o Tsunaideyuku." (Japanese: その夜は、みんなの思いを繋いでゆく。) October 26, 2015
Kyōko, Ayano, and Chitose have a sleepover at Yui's place, while Chinatsu, Sakurako, and Himawari spend the night at Akari's, where Tomoko is also spending the night with Akane.
5 "The Girl Falls Into Darkness" Transcription: "Shōjo wa, Yami ni Ochiru" (Japanese: 少女は、闇に落ちる) November 2, 2015
Kyōko, Yui, Ayano, and Chitose help teach Himawari how to do back hip circles in time for a test the next day.
6 "The Invisible Face is There." Transcription: "Mienai Kao wa, Soko ni Aru." (Japanese: 見えない顔は、其処にある。) November 9, 2015
Kyōko does all sorts of things to try to get Yui's attention before everyone tries their hand at apple peeling. Later, the girls find themselves in the middle of a blackout, where they have to deal with more of Kyōko's antics.
7 "It Will Be an Unforgettable Day" Transcription: "Wasurerarenai Ichinichi ni Naru" (Japanese: 忘れられない一日になる) November 16, 2015
Ayano tries to work up the courage to invite Kyōko to the movies with her, only to wind up invited by her instead.
8 "It Is the Piece of a Smile Everyone Acquires." Transcription: "Sore wa, Daremo ga Te ni suru Egao no Kakera." (Japanese: それは、誰もが手にする笑顔のカケラ。) November 23, 2015
Rise returns a dropped notebook to Akari, who gives her a cough drop in return, setting her on a Straw Millionaire-style series of exchanges as she helps out the other students.
9 "This is the Story of a Little Love and Courage" Transcription: "Sore wa, Chīsana Koi to, Sukoshi no Yūki no Monogatari." (Japanese: それは, 小さな恋と, 少しの勇気の物語。) November 30, 2015
Following her encounter with Kaede, Chizuru decides to try and better herself in order to become friends with her classmates Dezaki and Koyama.
10 "With You, Forever" Transcription: "Kimi to Nara Itsudemo" (Japanese: 君とならいつでも) December 7, 2015
Akari helps Yui to shovel snow in Mari's yard before everyone uses what's left to try and build a snow hut.
11 "Terrible No Matter How Much You Struggle" Transcription: "Dō Agaitemo Dotsubo" (Japanese: どうあがいても土壺) December 14, 2015
Yui and Ayano awkwardly try to make conversation while waiting for Kyōko and Chitose, constantly misunderstanding each other's reactions to their puns.
12 "Cherry Blossoms in Full Bloom and a Romantic Storm" Transcription: "Mankai Sakura ni Roman no Arashi" (Japanese: 満開桜に浪漫の嵐) December 21, 2015
The girls enjoy their flower viewing party, putting on skits and playing games, before being treated to a wonderful view of the falling flowers.
