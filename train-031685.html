Rank Title Notes
1. Mother India The film became a blockbuster at the box office, eventually becoming the highest-grossing film of the decade. It was directed by Mehboob Khan and starred Nargis, Sunil Dutt, Rajendra Kumar and Raaj Kumar.
2. Naya Daur Dilip Kumar and Vyjayantimala star in a drama, where the horse-cart drivers fight against the introduction of buses to the town, threatening their livelihoods.
3. Pyaasa Considered one of Guru Dutt's finest films, it stars him, Waheeda Rehman and Mala Sinha in a romantic drama about a poet who rebels against the norms of society and grows cynical of the world.
