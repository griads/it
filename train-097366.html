Table Section Text: Periodic free updates are released as content patches on an average of three per month since the game's release. These fix bugs, and add relatively small amounts of new content.
Title Year Type Notes
Notum Wars 2002 booster Notum Wars was the game's first major content update in 2002. It introduced the land control areas which are now a central gameplay feature.
Shadowlands 2003 expansion Shadowlands has been the largest expansion to date. Seven themed areas, and dozens of other new locations significantly added to the game's playable area.
Alien Invasion 2004 expansion Most of Alien Invasion's content centered around player organizations. "City plots" were placed throughout Rubi-Ka which organizations can buy to erect custom cities.
Lost Eden 2006 expansion Lost Eden was the first expansion released after the original story had ended. It focused almost entirely on player versus player combat, although new dynamic missions were also included.
Legacy of the Xan 2009 booster Legacy of the Xan focused on "end-game" content for existing players. It added new areas, equipment, and weapons for players whose characters have reached a high level.
