List of Ranma ½ episodes (season 7)


Section Title: Episode list (1992)
Table Section Text: None
Original Episode No. Viz Media Episode No. Title Original Air Date
119 137 "Tatewaki Kuno, Substitute Principal" Transcription: "Kunō Tatewaki, Dairi Kōchō wo Meizu" (Japanese: 九能帯刀, 代理校長を命ず) April 10, 1992
Principal Kuno elects Tatewaki Kuno to be the substitute principal as a test of leadership while he is away on vacation.
120 138 "Ranma's Greatest Challenge!?" Transcription: "Ranma, Tsukiyo ni Hoeru" (Japanese: 乱馬, 月夜に吠える) April 17, 1992
Ranma Saotome prepares to conquer the Ogre Festival of the Dark, a tournament resembling that of kick the can, in which Happosai personally trains him for this special event.
121 139 "Nihao! Jusenkyo Guide" Transcription: "Nihao! Jusenkyō no Gaido-san" (Japanese: 你好(ニーハオ)!呪泉郷のガイドさん) April 24, 1992
The Jusenkyo Guide travels to Japan in order perform a ceremony which will tap the water table that is the source of the Spring of the Drowned Man.
122 140 "Pick-a-Peck o' Happosai" Transcription: "Meiwaku! Rokunin no Happōsai" (Japanese: 迷惑!六人の八宝斉) May 1, 1992
Kasumi Tendo is given the occult tarot deck by Kuno. When Happosai is surrounded by the Seal of Solomon of the occult tarot deck, he is duplicated as six counterparts.
123 141 "From the Depths of Despair - Part 1" Transcription: "Kibun Shidai no Hissatsuwaza - Zen" (Japanese: 気分しだいの必殺技(前)) May 8, 1992
Ryoga Hibiki demonstrates the "Shishi Hōkōdan" technique on Ranma. It is then that Ranma tries to figure out the ki of this technique, in an attempt to counterattack.
124 142 "From the Depths of Despair - Part 2" Transcription: "Kibun Shidai no Hissatsuwaza - Kō" (Japanese: 気分しだいの必殺技(後)) May 15, 1992
To perfect the dangerous "Shishi Hōkōdan" technique, one must travel to the depths of despair. Ranma and Ryoga battle on school grounds, where the former struggles to find a flaw in the technique.
125 143 "Shampoo's Cursed Kiss" Transcription: "Shanpū Toraware no Kissu" (Japanese: シャンプー囚われのキッス) May 22, 1992
Shampoo goes to a temple to deliver food, but is reluctant to see the ghost cat there. She is spellbound by the ghost cat to be trapped in the temple.
126 144 "Run Away With Me, Ranma!" Transcription: "Boku to Kakeochi Shite kudasai" (Japanese: ボクと駆け落ちして下さい) May 29, 1992
Ranma experiences a recurring nightmare about encountering an old man named Harumaki in a field of blooming cherry blossoms, who has induced an out-of-body experience to search for his love interest Gyoko, to which Ranma in female form takes quite a resemblance.
127 145 "Let's Go to the Mushroom Temple" Transcription: "Kinoko Dera e Ikō" (Japanese: キノコ寺へ行こう) June 5, 1992
Everyone is invited to visit the Mushroom Temple, a place of fungal training and mushroom dining. At dinnertime Ranma, as well as Akane Tendo, are the only ones not poisoned by the mushroom soup.
128 146 "The Cradle from Hell" Transcription: "Hissatsu! Jigoku no Yurikago" (Japanese: 必殺!地獄のゆりかご) June 12, 1992
After challenging Ranma to a match, Genma Saotome has verified his lack in training. The two go their separate ways to train on their own, preparing for a second match a week later.
129 147 "Madame St. Paul's Cry for Help" Transcription: "Aoi Kyōfu ni Bonjūru" (Japanese: 青い恐怖にボンジュール) June 19, 1992
A vat of forbidden, blue wine is having a strange effect on Picolet Chardin, the masterful monsieur of martial-arts dining, having characteristics of a vampire.
130 148 "Meet You in the Milky Way" Transcription: "Orihime wa Nagareboshi ni Notte" (Japanese: 織姫は流れ星に乗って) June 26, 1992
Tanabata, a Japanese star festival held on the seventh day of the seventh month, celebrates the celestial reunion of the Princess and the Cowherd.
131 149 "Wretched Rice Cakes Of Love" Transcription: "Hitotsu Meshimase Koi no Sakuramochi" (Japanese: 一つ召しませ恋の桜餅) July 3, 1992
Akane is given a recipe for making sakuramochi to test out the theory of a salesperson foretelling her destiny in matrimony.
132 150 "The Horrible Happo Mold-Burst" Transcription: "Dekita! Happō Dai Kabin" (Japanese: できた!八宝大カビン) July 10, 1992
After the failure of the "Happo Daikarin" technique of explosions due to the heavy downpour, Happosai invents the "Happo Daikabin" technique, which releases clouds of paralytic mold.
133 151 "The Kuno Sibling Scandal" Transcription: "Kunō Kyōdai Sukyandaru no Arashi" (Japanese: 九能兄妹スキャンダルの嵐) July 17, 1992
Tatewaki Kuno and Kodachi Kuno are in sibling rivalry for their love interest. While Kuno is obsessed with Ranma in female form, Kodachi is infatuated with Ranma in male form.
134 152 "Battle for the Golden Tea Set" Transcription: "Ougon no Chaki, Gojōnotō no Kessen" (Japanese: 黄金の茶器, 五重塔の決戦) July 24, 1992
Sentaro Daimonji returns in need of Ranma and Akane's aid. It is explained that an opponent is planning to steal the golden teapot of enishi, that of which the rival schools have fought over for generations.
135 153 "Gosunkugi's Summer Affair" Transcription: "Gosunkugi Hikaru, Hito Natsu no Koi" (Japanese: 五寸釘光, ひと夏の恋) July 31, 1992
When Hikaru Gosunkugi meets Kogane Musashi for the first time, he instantly falls in love with her, later shamefully learning that she is a ghost.
136 155 "Bring It On! Love as a Cheerleader - Part 1" Transcription: "Ai no Kakutō Chiagāru - Zen" (Japanese: 愛の格闘チアガール(前)) August 7, 1992
Ranma is challenged by Mariko Konjo, the captain of the Seisyun High School cheerleading squad, to a martial arts cheerleading competition, in which Kuno is collateral.
137 156 "Bring It On! Love as a Cheerleader - Part 2" Transcription: "Ai no Kakutō Chiagāru - Kō" (Japanese: 愛の格闘チアガール(後)) August 14, 1992
Ranma and Mariko are to compete in martial arts cheerleading during the kendo club tournament, all to win the affection of Kuno.
138 154 "Battle for Miss Beachside" Transcription: "Kettei! Misu Bīchisaido" (Japanese: 決定!ミス·ビーチサイド) August 21, 1992
At the beach, Akane, Shampoo, and Ukyo Kuonji, want to cook a lunchtime meal for Ranma, so the three compete in a beauty contest to settle the conflict.
139 157 "The Musical Instruments of Destruction" Transcription: "Bakuretsu! Haipā Tsuzumi" (Japanese: 爆裂!ハイパーツヅミ) August 28, 1992
Kuno and Kodachi come across the ancient tsuzumi (drum) and biwa (lute), respectively, both having devastating destructive power when played. Kuno showcases the weaponry of his drum, destroying much of the school and putting Principal Kuno in depression.
140 158 "A Ninja's Dog is Black and White" Transcription: "Shinobi no Inu wa Shiro to Kuro" (Japanese: 忍の犬は白と黒) September 4, 1992
Ryoga encounters Shirokuro, a black and white runaway ninja dog, who later takes a love letter Ryoga had written out of his backpack and delivers it to Akane.
141 159 "The Tendo Dragon Legend" Transcription: "Tendō-ke: Ryūjin Densetsu" (Japanese: 天道家·龍神伝説) September 11, 1992
Ranma and Akane discover a seahorse in the ground on their way back home, in which Ranma decides to use it as a class project.
142 160 "Boy Meets Mom - Part 1" Transcription: "Ranma, Mītsu Mazā" (Japanese: 乱馬, ミーツ·マザー) September 18, 1992
Nodoka Saotome, Ranma's mother and Genma's wife, is planning to visit the Tendo household. It is revealed that Nodoka gave custody of Ranma to Genma.
143 161 "Boy Meets Mom - Part 2: Someday, Somehow..." Transcription: "Itsu no Hi ka, Kitto..." (Japanese: いつの日か, きっと...) September 25, 1992
Nodoka is planning to leave the Tendo household the following morning. Akane schedules an appointment between Ranma and Nodoka to meet in the nearby park, and later in a restaurant.
