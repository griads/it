15 "Jungle Fever" Larry Alexander September 20, 1989 192015

17 "The Fire of Hercufleas" Mark McCorkle and Robert Schooley September 26, 1989 192017

18 "Mario Meets Koop-zilla" Perry Martin October 19, 1989 192018

19 "Mario and Joliet" Michael A. Medlock and David Tischman October 11, 1989 192019

32 "20,000 Koopas Under the Sea" J. Larry Carroll and David Bennett Carren October 17, 1989 192032

37 "The Trojan Koopa" Mark McCorkle and Robert Schooley November 2, 1989 192037
