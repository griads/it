List of pre-statehood mayors of San Diego


Section Title: Alcaldes of the Pueblo of San Diego
Table Section Text: San Diego became a pueblo in 1834, after a petition to Governor José Figueroa endorsed by Commandant Santiago Arguello. The first Alcalde (mayor) Juan María Osuna was elected, defeating Pío Pico by 13 votes.
