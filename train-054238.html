Stechkin automatic pistol - 9×18mm Makarov Selective fire Machine pistol Soviet Union 20-round magazine, used by special forces.

SPS - 9×21mm Gyurza Semi-automatic pistol Russia 18-round magazine, used by special forces.
Assault rifles[edit]
OTs-14 Groza - 7.62×39mm Bullpup Assault rifle Russia 30-round magazine. Used with special and engineering forces.
AK-74 - 5.45×39mm Assault rifle Soviet Union Currently under modernization. 30-round magazine.
AK-74M - 5.45×39mm Assault rifle Russia 30-round magazine. Main service rifle of Russian Ground Forces.
AK-12 - 5.45×39mm Assault rifle Russia Completed operational testing in 2017. It has been adopted by the Russian Ground Forces in January 2018 under the Ratnik program.
AK-15 - 7.62×39mm Assault rifle Russia Completed operational testing in 2017. It has been adopted by the Russian Ground Forces in January 2018 under the Ratnik program.
A-545 - 5.45×39mm Assault rifle Russia Version of the AEK-971, entered operational service in 2018. Will see limited usage by special forces units only.
AN-94 - 5.45×39mm Assault rifle Russia 30-round magazine, new 60-round magazine, 2-round burst mode fired at 1800 RPM, originally designed to replace AK-74M, cancelled from general issue due to cost.
AS "Val" - 9×39mm Suppressed automatic rifle Soviet Union/ Russia 10- or 20-round magazine, uses an integrated suppressor. ASM variant introduced as part of the Ratnik infantry combat system.
SR-3M - 9×39mm Suppressed automatic rifle Russia 20- or 30-round magazine.

Dragunov SVD - 7.62×54mmR Semi-automatic sniper rifle/designated marksman rifle Soviet Union / Russia 10-round magazine, semi auto, main service sniper rifle. Russian-origin 9.3×64mm SVDK variant is used for dealing with targets behind heavy cover, while SVU variant is used by special forces.
SV-98 - 7.62×54mmR Bolt-action sniper rifle Russia 10-round removable magazine, iron and telescopic sights
SVDM - 7.62×54mmR Semi-automatic sniper rifle/designated marksman rifle Russia 10-round magazine, semi auto. Introduced as part of the Ratnik infantry combat system.
ASVK-M Kord-M - 12.7×108mm Anti-materiel rifle Russia 5-round magazine, bolt action. Introduced as part of the Ratnik infantry combat system.
VSS "Vintorez" Vss vintorez 01.jpeg 9×39mm Suppressed sniper rifle Soviet Union/ Russia 10- or 20-round magazine, semi or full auto, uses an integrated suppressor. VSSM variant introduced as part of the Ratnik infantry combat system.
ASVK - 12.7×108mm Anti-materiel rifle Russia 5-round magazine, bolt action.
OSV-96 - 12.7×108mm Anti-materiel rifle Russia 5-round magazine, semi auto, can be folded in half, limited usage.

MRO-A - 72.5mm Rocket-propelled grenade Russia Single-shot, disposable rocket launcher. Uses MRO-A thermobaric, MRO-D smoke-creating and MRO-Z incendiary rockets.

RPG-22 - 72.5mm Rocket-propelled grenade Soviet Union Single-shot disposable launcher.
RPG-26 - 72.5mm Rocket-propelled grenade Soviet Union / Russia Single-shot disposable launcher and Russian-origin RShG-2 thermobaric warhead for anti-infantry use.
RPG-27 - 105mm Rocket-propelled grenade Soviet Union / Russia Single-shot disposable launcher with tandem-charge warhead in an anti-tank role, RShG-1 thermobaric warhead for anti-infantry use and RMG variant for bunker busting use entered service in 2011.
RPG-28 - 125 mm Rocket-propelled grenade Russia Single-shot disposable launcher with tandem charge warhead.
RPG-29 RPG-29 USGov.JPG 105mm Rocket-propelled grenade Soviet Union Re-loadable launcher. Can use PG-29V tandem-charge munitions in an anti-tank role, or TBG-29 thermobaric munitions in anti-infantry use.
RPG-30 - 105mm Rocket-propelled grenade Russia Single-shot disposable launcher for countering active protection systems.
RPG-32 105mm Rocket-propelled grenade Russia Re-loadable launcher. Can use PG-32V tandem-charge munitions in an anti-tank role, or TBG-32V thermobaric munitions in anti-infantry use.
