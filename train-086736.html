List of RahXephon episodes


Section Title: Episode list
Table Section Text: None
No. Title Writer Original air date
1 "Invasion of the Capital - Overlord" Transcription: "Shuto Shinkō" (Japanese: 首都侵攻 OVER LORD) Yutaka Izubuchi January 21, 2002
Ayato Kamina, Hiroko Asahina, and Mamoru Torigai are on their way to a chemistry test by train with a woman who had been following Ayato.
2 "God and Man Awaken - Awakening" Transcription: "Shinjin Mezameru" (Japanese: 神人目覚める AWAKENING) Fumihiko Takayama January 28, 2002
The RahXephon is summoned by Ayato and finishes defeating the invaders before defeating the Dolem named Fortissimo in battle. Ayato is then taken home by his mother Maya Kamina, who insists that there were no such things floating in the sky.
3 "City of Two - Welcome to our town" Transcription: "Futari no Machi" (Japanese: 二人の街 Welcome to our town) Yukari Kiryu February 4, 2002
Ayato is hindered in his escape by Fortissimo. The stalker, now recognized as Haruka Shitou, is held safe by the RahXephon, but Reika stands on its shoulder as it is hit by an energy blast.
4 "His Own Watch - Watch the year hand" Transcription: "Jibun no Tokei" (Japanese: 自分の時計 Watch the year hand) Yoji Enokido February 18, 2002
Ayato is transported by aircraft carrier to the base of the TERRA Organization. Although he is kindly introduced to Quon Kisaragi and Makoto Isshiki, Ayato is locked up because he is considered a threat.
5 "Nirai-Kanai - On Earth As It Is In Heaven" Transcription: "Niraikanai" (Japanese: ニライカナイ On Earth As It Is In Heaven) Yoji Enokido February 25, 2002
Ayato's distressed feelings causes the RahXephon to petrify. Itsuki Kisaragi and Souichi Yakumo come up with a plan to resolve the situation.
6 "Obliterated Cities - Lost Songs Forgotten Memories" Transcription: "Shōmetsu Toshi" (Japanese: 消滅都市 Lost Songs Forgotten Melodies) Ichiro Okouchi March 4, 2002
Jin Kunugi and Shirou Watari attend a press conference, leaving Souichi in charge of operations. After Kim Hotal insults Ayato for being useless in the organization, Megumi explains to Ayato that Kim's parents were killed by a Dolem in Australia.
7 "Day of Assembly - Phantom In The Cloud" Transcription: "Atsumaru Hi" (Japanese: 集まる日 Phantom In The Cloud) Ichiro Okouchi March 11, 2002
Kunugi, Watari, and Makoto bring with them Johji Futagami, who wants to start an exclusive investigation on the TERRA Organization, and he is sent to Nirai-Kanai for observations.
8 "Bitterly Cold Holy Night - The Dreaming Stone" Transcription: "Kōru Seiya" (Japanese: 凍る聖夜 The Dreaming Stone) Yoji Enokido March 18, 2002
Itsuki gives out an invitation for a Christmas party at his place at night. Snow has begun to fall for the first time on Nirai-Kanai.
9 "Small Shrine of Time - Sanctuary" Transcription: "Toki no Hokora" (Japanese: 時 の 祠 SANCTUARY) Sho Aikawa March 25, 2002
On New Year's Day, Ayato, Quon, and Haruka go to the beach. While on a boat trip, after Haruka takes a dive into the water, Ayato and Quon glimpse at Reika near a lighthouse.
S "Memory of Wing - Memory" Transcription: "Tokubetsu Henshūban Tsubasa no Kioku" (Japanese: 翼の記憶 Memory) TBA April 10, 2002
A recapitulation episode aired after the series break that coincides with the 1 April start of a new Japanese fiscal year.
10 "Sonata of Reminiscence - War In The Remembrance" Transcription: "Tsuioku no Sonata" (Japanese: 追憶のソナタ War In The remembrance) Yukari Kiryu April 17, 2002
Kunugi takes a rare day off, and Quon seems to be forgetting something at his place. When Ayato listens to a recording of Quon playing the violin, he senses a vibe of dissonance.
11 "[Kyoja] Circuit - Nightmare" Transcription: "Kyoja Kairo" (Japanese: 虚邪回路 NightMare) Chiaki J. Konaka April 24, 2002
Ayato struggles inside the RahXephon as a Dolem begin to swallow him up. He somehow briefly encounters Hiroko and Mamoru in an arcade, but the former tries to make a move on him, causing him to run off.
12 "The Black Egg - Resonance" Transcription: "Kuroi Tamago" (Japanese: 黒い卵 Resonance) Chiaki J. Konaka May 1, 2002
Quon, having been in a comatose state, opens her eyes after Itsuki and Sayoko leave the laboratory. Ayato takes a walk with Futagami, who shows him a picture of Maya as the leader of the Mulians.
13 "Human Specimen #1 - Sleeping Beauty" Transcription: "Ningen Hyōhon Dai Ichi Gō" (Japanese: 人間標本第1号 Sleeping Beauty) Ichiro Okouchi May 8, 2002
A Dolem is seen to be in orbit in the sky, but seems immobile. Sayoko is curious to know how and why Quon appeared in the RahXephon with Ayato.
14 "The Boy In The Mirror - Time After Time" Transcription: "Kagami no Naka no Shōnen" (Japanese: 鏡の中の少年 Time After Time) Hiroshi Ohnogi May 22, 2002
Helena von Bähbem has learned more about Quon's state of awakening than what Itsuki had intended. Futagami informs Haruka that Rikudo had a unknown daughter that had left Tokyo at seventeen years of age, changing her family name to Kamina.
15 "The Children's Night - Childhood's End" Transcription: "Kodomotachi no Yoru" (Japanese: 子供たちの夜 Child Hood's End) Mitsuo Iso May 29, 2002
A flashback occurs to when Makoto, Itsuki, and Helena were being raised at the Bähbem manor. Makoto finds a Mud Doll underground and decides to take it back to its mother and father, though Itsuki has his doubts and Helena is against the matter.
16 "Island of Others - The Moon Princess" Transcription: "Tanin no Shima" (Japanese: The Moon Princess 他人の島) Hiroshi Ohnogi June 5, 2002
Megumi is looking forward to a promotion and to confess her love to Souichi. Ayato struggles to cope with the fact that Kunugi and Watari concealed the fact of him being a Mulian.
17 "Return to the Labyrinth - Ground Zero" Transcription: "Meikyū he no Kikan" (Japanese: 迷宮への帰還 Ground Zero) Chiaki J. Konaka June 12, 2002
Elvy and Haruka go out in the Vermilion to intercept Ayato and Quon, but a Dolem appears and fights the Vermilion, and they are unable to stop Ayato and Quon.
18 "The Bond of Blue Blood - The Memory of a Lost City" Transcription: "Aoki Chi no Kizuna" (Japanese: 蒼き血の絆 The Memory Of A Lost City) Chiaki J. Konaka June 19, 2002
Ayato, horrified of the sight of blue blood emitting from Maya, unknowing causes the RahXephon to act on its own at the base.
19 "Blue Friend - Ticket To Nowhere" Transcription: "Burū Furendo" (Japanese: ブルーフレンド Ticket To Nowhere) Fumihiko Takayama June 26, 2002
Ayato and Hiroko escape from Tokyo Jupiter, however Futagami is following in their footsteps. Hiroko is too scared to tell Ayato about her blood turning blue.
20 "[The Battle of One Who Manipulates] - Interested Parties" Transcription: "Ayanasu Hito no Tatakai" (Japanese: 綾なす人の戦い Interested Parties) Yoji Enokido July 3, 2002
Makoto replaces Kunugi as commander, and brings with him a new staff member, who so happens to be Reika. Mamoru seems to have successfully escaped from Tokyo, finding Ayato and meeting Megumi.
21 "The Carved Seal of Xephon - Goodbye My Friend" Transcription: "Zefon no Kokuin" (Japanese: ゼフォンの刻印 Good Bye My Friend) Hiroshi Ohnogi July 31, 2002
Souichi and Haruka draws out Makoto's plans for another operation to break the barrier of Tokyo Jupiter. Quon is again settled in at the Bähbem Foundation.
22 "Operation Jupiter Obliteration - Downfall" Transcription: "Mokusei Shōmetsu Sakusen" (Japanese: 木星消滅作戦 Down Fall) Hiroshi Ohnogi August 7, 2002
Makoto, after firing Haruka from the organization on behalf of Ayato's recent acts of theft, orders the operation to start one hour sooner than scheduled.
23 "[From Here To Eternity] - Where The Sweet Bird Song" Transcription: "Koko Yori Towa ni" (Japanese: ここより永遠に Where The Sweet Bird Song) Ichiro Okouchi August 14, 2002
Kunugi returns back the base as commander, and Haruka regains her position as well. Masayoshi Kuki threatens the TERRA Organization to hand over the RahXephon to him within one day or be faced against an approaching Dolem named Largo.
24 "Doorway to the Tuning - Twin Music" Transcription: "Chōritsu he no Tobira" (Japanese: 調律への扉 Twin Music) Chiaki J. Konaka August 21, 2002
All citizens and noncombatant passengers are to evacuate off the base, while Souichi is to lead his fleet unit into combat.
25 "God's Uncertain Music - Deus Ex Machina" Transcription: "Kami no Futashika na Oto" (Japanese: 神の不確かな音 Deus Ex Machina) Chiaki J. Konaka August 28, 2002
The RahXephon blasts out a rainbow wave directly at Obbligato from the heavens, sending a mass destruction all over the city.
26 "Far Beyond Eternity - Time Enough For Love" Transcription: "Haruka Kuon no Kanata" (Japanese: 遙か久遠の彼方) Hiroshi Ohnogi September 11, 2002
Ayato wakes up in the subway station when Quon is playing the piano. Itsuki is stabbed to death by Sayoko, but he dies contently knowing she killed him.
