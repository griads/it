List of Spider-Man enemies


Section Title: The Amazing Spider-Man debuts
Table Section Text: Most of the supervillains of Spider-Man would be introduced in The Amazing Spider-Man comic book starting with the Chameleon. The early villains would be introduced in the 1960s in the Silver Age of Comic Books, and created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko.

Lizard Dr. Curt Connors #6 (November 1963) Stan Lee Steve Ditko Dr. Curt Connors tested himself an experimental serum from reptile DNA which created him as a giant humanoid Lizard.
