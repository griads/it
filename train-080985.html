List of Maverick episodes

Table Section Text: Writer/creator Roy Huggins leaves the show. Garner and Kelly remain the leads.

Pappy Bret Bart
The Mavericks set out to investigate when it appears that an 18-year-old gold-digger is set to marry their Pappy. Features dual roles for series stars Garner and Kelly, as "Pappy" Beauregard Maverick and Uncle Bentley, respectively.
Royal Four-Flush Bart

The Sheriff of Duck 'n' Shoot Bret Bart
Bret is manipulated into becoming the sheriff of a wildly boisterous town, and tries to outfox—rather than outgun—both the criminals and the town officials who placed him in the job.
You Can't Beat the Percentage Bart
A cowboy is set to kill the new boss of one of Bart's old girlfriends, who now works at a gambling house.
The Cats of Paradise Bret
Bret gets involved with Modesty Blaine (Mona Freeman) in a scheme to sell cats to a town plagued with rats. There he faces Buddy Ebsen as a trigger-happy sheriff, Richard Deacon as the town's undertaker, and Lance Fuller as a black-clad business-card carrying gunfighter modeled on Paladin.
A Tale of Three Cities Bart
Bart is held up at gunpoint by a female robber (Pat Crowley), and then is kicked out of town by the sheriff.
Full House Bret
Bret is mistaken for one Foxy Smith, who is assembling ten of the West's most notorious outlaws for a major heist.
The Lass With the Poisonous Air Bart
While winning regularly at a series of private Denver poker games, Bart leaves promptly every afternoon for a rendezvous with a mysterious femme fatale (Joanna Moore).
The Ghost Soldiers Bret
An extremely beleaguered Bret must figure out some way to cope with an ocean of Native Americans laying siege to an almost-empty fort.
Easy Mark Bart
Bart's paid handsomely to impersonate cactus-fancier Cornelius Van Rennselaer Jr. on a railroad journey, but he doesn't realize that others on the train will stop at nothing to keep "Cornelius" from completing his trip.
A Fellow's Brother Bret Bart
Bret is wrongly identified as a wanted thief and killer, and must contend with a bumbling sheriff and bounty hunter who are determined to capture him for the reward money, as well as vengeful family members of the killer's victim ...
Trooper Maverick Bart
Caught gambling in a frontier fort, Bart forgoes 180 days in the stockade by enlisting in the cavalry—with a secret mission to expose a traitorous ring of thieves.
Maverick Springs Bret Bart
Bret is hired by a wealthy Texas woman to bring her wayward brother home from Saratoga Springs. The project soon gets trickier than Bret anticipated, and eventually brother Bart is called in to help.
The Goose-Drownder Bart Gentleman Jack
Bart and Gentleman Jack Darby are stranded in a decaying Nevada ghost town during a week-long downpour (a "goose-drownder") -- but the tedium is broken when a stagecoach turns up carrying a desperate gang, including one of Bart's lost loves (Fay Spain).
A Cure for Johnny Rain Bret
Bret and his travelling companions are held up at gunpoint on a stagecoach. Later, when Bret arrives in the frontier town of Apocalypse, he encounters the thief again: Johnny Rain (William Reynolds), the most beloved citizen in town.
The Marquesa Bart
Adele Mara guest stars as a woman with a competing claim on a saloon which Bart wins in a poker game.
The Cruise of the Cynthia B Bret Bart
Bret is conned into buying an almost worthless—and seemingly cursed—riverboat. Mona Freeman returns as Modesty Blaine, another victim of the same conman.
Maverick and Juliet Bret Bart
En route to visit Pappy in St. Louis, Bret runs afoul of two families of feuding Missouri hillbillies.
The White Widow Bart
After Bart's entire bankroll is stolen from a hotel safe, he accepts a position as a bodyguard for widowed bank president Wilma White (Julie Adams) who has been receiving death threats.
Guatemala City Bret
Bret searches for an ex-girlfriend in tropical Guatemala and befriends a female street urchin. With Suzanne Storrs and Patric Knowles.
The People's Friend Bart
After foiling an assassination attempt on a state senator, Bart is convinced to run for election in his place while the senator recovers from the bullet wound he received.
A Flock of Trouble Bret
Bret wins a herd of sheep in a poker game, thinking they're cattle.....and soon discovers that the locals have an absolute murderous hatred of sheep farmers.
The Iron Hand Bart
Having lost everything to Nobby Ned Wingate (Edward Ashley) in a poker game, Bart is forced to find work for himself and his disgruntled companions on a cattle drive.
The Resurrection of Joe November Bret
Bret accepts a well-paying job from an old family friend to dig up a grave, so that the body may be interred elsewhere—but the job soon becomes much more complex, and dangerous.
The Misfortune Teller Bret
Bret rides in to a small Wyoming town where he's never been before, and is immediately thrown into jail, accused of having killed the town's mayor.
Greenbacks, Unlimited Bret
Bret is hired to track down a safecracker who is believed to be getting ready to rob the Denver State Bank.
