Teso language


Section Title: Noun prefix
Table Section Text: Every noun in Ateso has a prefix which varies according to the gender of the noun or according to whether the noun is singular or plural.
masculine feminine neuter
singular e,o a i
plural i,o a i
e.g. etelepat → itelepai (boy → boys) apese → apesur (girl → girls) ikoku → idwe (child → children)

Sentence(s)

1. All nouns in Ateso are divided into genders:(a) masculine, (b) feminine and (c) neuter.
