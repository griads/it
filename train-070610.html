List of Beavis and Butt-Head episodes


Section Title: Season 3 (1993–94)
Table Section Text: None
No. overall No. in season Title Original air date
32 1 "Comedians" September 6, 1993
The two go to a comedy club where Butt-Head's stand-up comedy fails. Beavis sets fire to the comedy club after burning newspapers to juggle with.
33 2 "Carwash" September 6, 1993
The two are hired to wash a Chevrolet Corvair in order to pay for new TV remote batteries, but decide to take it for a ride.
34 3 "Couch-Fishing" September 7, 1993
The two search their couch for interesting items to use as bait to fish out of their window onto the street while they sit on their couch.
35 4 "Incognito" September 8, 1993
Under threat of possible death from a gun-toting student, the duo assume disguises and pretend to be exchange students. Featured videos: Run–D.M.C.
36 5 "Kidnapped" September 8, 1993
The duo conceive a plot to "kidnap" clueless Stewart for ransom. Featured videos: Kix – "Cool Kids" Kylie Minogue – "Locomotion" Raging Slab – "Anywhere but Here" White Zombie – "Welcome to Planet Motherfucker"
37 6 "Kidnapped, Part II" September 8, 1993
"Kidnapping" Stewart proves to be more than the pair bargained for. Featured videos: Led Zeppelin – "Over the Hills and Far Away" The Shamen – "Ebeneezer Goode" Blondie – "Rapture" Beastie Boys – "Gratitude"
38 7 "Naked Colony" September 13, 1993
Beavis and Butt-head, upon discovering the concept of a nudist colony, attempt to join one. They cannot afford the membership fee, so they climb over the wall.
39 8 "Tornado" September 14, 1993
Upon hearing of a coming tornado, the duo decide to investigate at a trailer park and meet two trailer trash girls who want to have sex with two men before dying in the storm.
40 9 "Cleaning House" September 20, 1993
Beavis and Butt-head are hired for a job, this time by their teacher, David Van Driessen, and the duo end up destroying his entire 8-track tape collection.
41 10 "Scratch 'n' Win" September 21, 1993
The duo get a dollar and win $500 by buying a lottery ticket. They then buy a riding mower, which they use to cut a circle-A in the lawn in front of Highland High School.
42 11 "Scared Straight" September 27, 1993
McVicker restarts a Scared Straight program and the duo find themselves having fun in prison. Featured videos: Judas Priest – "Breaking the Law" Plasmatics – "The Damned" The Dead Milkmen – "Punk Rock Girl" AC/DC – "Highway to Hell" U2 – "Lemon"
43 12 "Eating Contest" September 30, 1993
Beavis and Butt-head enter a bratwurst-eating contest. They are unaware of and unable to pay the entrance fee, so they have to work with frogs to pay it.
44 13 "Sporting Goods" October 4, 1993
Beavis and Butt-head are sent by an angry Coach Buzzcut to the local shop because they need to buy athletic supporters (or as they call them "sip otters") for gym class.
45 14 "Sperm Bank" October 7, 1993
The duo sell sperm, at the offices of Dr. Rod Johnson, and buy porn magazines with the money.
46 15 "Buff 'n' Stuff" October 14, 1993
Disgusted with their pathetic physiques, Buzzcut decides to enlist the duo in a weight training course. Featured videos: Milli Vanilli – "Baby Don't Forget My Number" Journey – "Separate Ways (Worlds Apart)" Onyx featuring Biohazard – "Slam" Eddie Money – "Shakin'"
47 16 "Citizen Butt-head" October 18, 1993
Bill Clinton is about to visit Highland, so McVicker does his best to keep Beavis and Butt-head preoccupied. He sends the boys off-campus.
48 17 "Citizen Butt-head, Part II" October 18, 1993
Bill Clinton visits Highland, and encounters Beavis and Butt-head, as well as Daria, during an assembly. Featured videos: Rollins Band – "Low Self Opinion" Suzanne Vega – "Blood Makes Noise" The Georgia Satellites – "Keep Your Hands to Yourself"
49 18 "Politically Correct" October 21, 1993
The duo accidentally run for student office - and receive one vote. Featured videos: Yoko Ono – "My Man" Metallica – "One" The Beloved – "Sweet Harmony"
50 19 "Ball Breakers" October 25, 1993
The duo take to bowling as their latest activity, While there, they steal Tom Anderson's bowling ball, put firecrackers in it and drop it from the roof of the building onto the street.
51 20 "Meet God" October 28, 1993
The two try to score chicks while hitchhiking—and unwittingly join a cult. Featured videos: Helloween – "Halloween" The Dickies – "Killer Klowns" The Sisters of Mercy – "Doctor Jeep" Electric Sun – "The Night the Master Comes" Prong – "Prove You Wrong"
52 21 "Meet God, Part II" October 28, 1993
The duo end up at the cult of a man claiming to be God. Featured videos: Alice Cooper – "Welcome to My Nightmare" Bauhaus – "Ziggy Stardust" Madonna – "Fever" Bananarama – "Venus"
53 22 "True Crime" November 1, 1993
The duo arrive at a local bank, where they find that someone has left their bank card in the ATM. Beavis correctly guesses the card's pin code, and the duo pocket thousands of dollars from the person's account.
54 23 "The Trial" November 4, 1993
After being locked up for egging Tom Anderson's house, Beavis and Butt-head face trial. However, they have no defence, except from Butthead, who relies on his viewing of the Pupils' Court as a way of defence.
55 24 "The Crush" November 8, 1993
The two attempt to join Todd's gang, and are made to ride together in the trunk of his car as an initiation test; afterwards he rejects them.
56 25 "Plate Frisbee" November 11, 1993
Beavis and Butt-head go to Stewart's house, where they pick up an antique plate belonging to Stewart's mother. They go outside and use it as a frisbee.
57 26 "Canoe" November 15, 1993
Van Driessen takes the duo and Stewart on a canoe trip. At their camp, Beavis and Butt-Head use poison ivy as toilet paper and a bear attacks Van Driessen.
58 27 "Young, Gifted, & Crude" November 25, 1993
The duo ace a placement test by filling out the multiple choice answers at random and are moved up to a gifted class.
59 28 "Foreign Exchange" December 9, 1993
The duo befriend a foreign exchange student from Japan. They make him as dumb as they are, which greatly disappoints his parents when he returns to Japan.
CS1 29 "A Very Special Christmas With Beavis and Butt-head" December 17, 1993
Extended episode. A Christmas-themed episode, featuring all music videos.
60 30 "Closing Time" December 23, 1993
Left in charge of Burger World after closing, Beavis and Butt-head throw burgers across the restaurant. A health inspector arrives, and is disgusted by the state of it.
61 31 "Most Wanted" March 5, 1994
Extended episode. The two are confronted by an escaped killer and end up with tattoos.
