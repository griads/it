List of The Backyardigans episodes


Section Title: Season 3 (2008–09)
Table Section Text: None
No. overall No. in season Title Directed by Written by Original air date
41 1 "Who Goes There?" Dave Palmer Chris Nee March 3, 2008
Tyrone is a night watchman at a museum where the exhibits (Lady Uniqua, a character from an Impressionist painting; General Pablo, a statue; and Austocles (Austin), a figure on a Greek urn) come to life at night.
42 2 "Blazing Paddles" Dave Palmer Rodney Stringfellow March 10, 2008
Uniqua is the sheriff of Ping Pong Mesa, a small western town. Tyrone is the town saloon barkeep, Tasha is the bar-room dancer, and Austin is the deputy.
43 3 "Garbage Trek" Dave Palmer Robert Scull March 14, 2008
In this Star Trekepisode, Lieutenant Uniqua, Captain Tasha, and Ensign Austin of the U.S.S. Collector roam the galaxy collecting space garbage.
44 4 "Fly Girl" Dave Palmer Chris Nee March 17, 2008
Uniqua is a pilot who delivers singing telegrams to three grumpy residents (a pirate played by Pablo, a maharani played by Tasha, and Tyrone the abominable snowman).
45 5 "What's Bugging You?" Dave Palmer Adam Peltzman April 4, 2008
Uniqua and Tyrone are pest controllers who deal with wild creatures by talking to them instead of exterminating them. Lady Tasha hires them to rid her house of Wormans before Mr.
46 6 "Chichen-Itza Pizza" Dave Palmer Robert Scull April 25, 2008
Uniqua and Tasha own a pizzeria in Chichen-Itza. After receiving an order from Mayan King Tyrone, they must cross the Mexican jungle to bring a pizza to his pyramid.
47 7 "To the Center of the Earth" Dave Palmer Billy Aronson May 2, 2008
Tyrone has dropped his lucky penny into the center of the earth. Victorian-era inventors Uniqua and Pablo decide that it must have fallen to the center of the earth and use their rocket-drill to find it.
48 8 "Front Page News" Dave Palmer Scott Gray May 9, 2008
Tasha is the photographer for Bigopolis Big News. A giant robot is inadvertently causing trouble in the city and her boss, Pablo the editor, asks her to get a picture of him and resident superheroes Bug Girl (Uniqua) and Captain Bubble (Tyrone).
49 9 "Tale of the Mighty Knights: Part 1" Dave Palmer Adam Peltzman July 7, 2008
Knights Uniqua and Tyrone are appointed to guard King Pablo’s egg. They chase it through the woods and meet the Grabbing Goblin (Austin), who attempts to steal the egg and add it to his collection of junk.
50 10 "Tale of the Mighty Knights: Part 2" Dave Palmer Adam Peltzman July 7, 2008
Knight Uniqua, Sir Tyrone, and the Grabbing Goblin encounter the Flighty Fairy (Tasha) after losing King Pablo's egg. The egg, which has sprouted wings, leads them to the dreaded Dragon Mountain.
51 11 "Le Master of Disguise" Dave Palmer Janice Burgess July 14, 2008
Inspector Austin of the Paris police is chasing down Le Master of Disguise (Pablo), whom he has cornered on the Orient Express bound for Istanbul.
52 12 "Match on Mt. Olympus" Dave Palmer Robert Scull July 18, 2008
Pablo and Tyrone (the weatherman and sportscaster on an Ancient Greek news station) forecast sunny weather that is perfect for playing basketball.
53 13 "The Great Dolphin Race" Dave Palmer Rodney Stringfellow July 21, 2008
Under the sea in Atlantis, Uniqua and Pablo run a dolphin stable for their rude employer and resident dolphin racer, Tyrone.
54 14 "Caveman's Best Friend" Dave Palmer Adam Peltzman July 25, 2008
Austin the caveman wants a pet, so he visits Uniqua's pet store and gets a dinosaur puppy named Boy. Uniqua warns him that Boy should always have his leash on, but Austin is too far away to hear her.
55 15 "Ranch Hands from Outer Space" Dave Palmer Rodney Stringfellow September 22, 2008
Overworked rancher Tasha wants to hire some ranch hands to help her out. Aliens Zuniqua and Zablo (Uniqua and Pablo) have landed their ship just outside her farm.
56 16 "Robin Hood the Clean" Dave Palmer Scott Gray September 26, 2008
Uniqua, Tyrone, and Tasha live in the perpetually dirty town of Filthingham. They wish to be clean, but Mayor Austin Stinkypants has forbidden cleanliness and locked all of their cosmetic products away.
57 17 "Escape from Fairytale Village" Dave Palmer Billy Aronson September 29, 2008
Tyrone the paperboy has a new route through the forest in Fairytale Village. He meets Uniqua the witch (from Hansel and Gretel tale), Pablo the giant (from Jack and the Beanstalk tale), and Austin the wolf (from The Three Little Pigs tale).
58 18 "Pirate Camp" Dave Palmer Kerri Grant October 6, 2008
Uniqua and a visibly nervous Pablo attend Captain Austin's pirate camp. Aboard his ship, Captain Austin instructs the campers in the fundamentals of pirating.
59 19 "The Two Musketeers" Dave Palmer Robert Scull October 10, 2008
Pablo and Tyrone are the Two Musketeers. They fight against the Empress, whose guards (Uniqua and Austin) pursue them.
60 20 "The Masked Retriever" Dave Palmer Janice Burgess October 13, 2008
Uniqua loves her job as a librarian in Viejo, California. When the evil Don Austin refuses to return a book, she becomes the Masked Retriever and tracks him down.
