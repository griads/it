List of The Love Boat episodes

Table Section Text: None
No. in series No. in season Title Original air date
137 1 "Venetian Love Song / Down for the Count / Arrividerci, Gopher / The Arrangement – Part 1" October 2, 1982
The crew goes to Italy: Dominic (Ernest Borgnine) and Teresa (Shelley Winters), originally from Italy, are bickering to the point of possible separation.
138 2 "Venetian Love Song / Down for the Count / Arrividerci, Gopher / The Arrangement – Part 2" October 2, 1982
The ship cruises to Rome, Capri and Venice. Teresa and Dominic decide to separate.
139 3 "The Anniversary Gift / Honey Bee Mine / Bewigged, Bothered and Bewildered" October 16, 1982
A couple celebrating their 20th anniversary (Robert Mandan and Lynn Redgrave) find that even with grown children, she still wants another baby.
140 4 "The Same Wavelength / Winning Isn't Everything / A Honeymoon for Horace" October 23, 1982
A psychic (Connie Stevens) comes on board to entertain the passengers and she meets a man (Charles Siebert) who's unsure if he wants to remain with the wife he's currently separated from his.
141 5 "Command Performance / Hyde and Seek / Sketchy Love" October 30, 1982
A young girl (Kim Richards) who is infatuated with Gopher pretends to be her older sister. An artist (Morgan Brittany) meets an unhappily married guy (Skip Stephenson).
142 6 "The Groupies / The Audition / Doc's Nephew" November 6, 1982
A psychiatrist (Richard Deacon) who specializes in group therapy goes on board for a little vacation but one of his groups joins him.
143 7 "The Spoonmaker Diamond / Papa Doc / The Role Model / Julie's Tycoon – Part 1" November 13, 1982
The crew sails on the Stella Solaris to Greece and Turkey: The priceless Spoonmaker Diamond is stolen from the Topkapi Museum.
144 8 "The Spoonmaker Diamond / Papa Doc / The Role Model / Julie's Tycoon – Part 2" November 13, 1982
Cliff and Monica renew their romance. Meanwhile, Cliff wants Vicki to model for him on an ongoing basis, which could start a career for her.
145 9 "The Best of Friends / Too Many Dads / Love Will Find a Way" November 20, 1982
It is Thanksgiving, and among the passengers is a woman (Wendy Schaal) who wants to introduce her fiancé (Jim Knaub) to her parents (Lorne Greene and Dorothy McGuire) but she neglected to tell them that he is a paraplegic.
146 10 "The Man in the Iron Shorts / The Victims / Heavens to Betsy" November 27, 1982
Gopher returns from vacation with a new girlfriend, history teacher Gail Cowler (Taylor Miller), who is a history teacher. He also has a trunk carrying a suit of armor, which he dons in order to impress her.
147 11 "The Tomorrow Lady / Father, Dear Father / Still Life" December 4, 1982
Glen Leciter (Howard Duff) meets Alice Bailey (Greer Garson), a woman who, by her own admission, is a "good guesser." He plans to use her clairvoyant gift to win big in the stock market.
148 12 "Baby Talk / My Friend, the Executrix / Programmed for Love" December 11, 1982
Connie Pierce (Donna Pescow) feels left out when her husband Steve (Grant Goodeve) takes a liking to Jan Willis (Angela Cartwright), who is to be their baby's surrogate mother.
149 13 "The Christmas Presence" December 18, 1982
On a Christmas cruise, a senior citizen named Angelarum Dominicus (Mickey Rooney) is present in the lives of several other passengers.
150 14 "Paroled to Love / First Impressions / Love Finds Florence Nightingale" January 8, 1983
This cruise is supposed to have some celebrities among the passengers. But the PR employees (Debbie Reynolds and Marilyn Michaels) booked them on a different cruise.
151 15 "The Captain's Replacement / Sly as a Fox / Here Comes the Bride – Maybe" January 15, 1983
Capt. Stubing mentors Captain Donahue (McLean Stevenson), whom Gopher thinks is out to take command of the Pacific Princess.
152 16 "Doc's Big Case / Senior Sinners / A Booming Romance" January 22, 1983
Doc's medical school chum, Dr. Elliot Norton (James Noble), gives Vicki some information for an article on Doc.
153 17 "Gopher's Daisy / Our Son, the Lawyer / Salvaged Romance" January 29, 1983
Gopher buys a Daisy exercise machine to improve his physique but doesn't want the other crew members to know about it.
154 18 "Isaac's Aegean Affair / The Captain and the Kid / Poor Rich Man / The Dean and the Flunkee – Part 1" February 5, 1983
The crew are in Greece working a cruise a college is having its graduation ceremony aboard: The dean (Eddie Albert) is attracted to a teacher (Shirley Jones) who rebuffs him because he won't give a student a make-up test so he can graduate.
155 19 "Isaac's Aegean Affair / The Captain and the Kid / Poor Rich Man / The Dean and the Flunkee – Part 2" February 5, 1983
Vicki falls in love, while a beloved crew member decides to leave the ship.
156 20 "The Zinging Valentine / The Very Temporary Secretary / Final Score" February 12, 1983
A woman who delivers singing telegrams (Claudia Lonow) ends up stuck aboard after delivering a "Dear John" message to a passenger (Donny Most).
157 21 "The Captain's Crush / Out of My Hair / Off-Course Romance" February 19, 1983
Movie star Janine Adams (Joan Collins), who has been divorced from nine husbands, boards the Princess for a cruise without knowing that a tabloid reporter is in tow to write a story about her love life.
158 22 "I Like to Be in America / He Ain't Heavy / Abby's Maiden Voyage" February 26, 1983
After being let go by her employer, April Lopez (Charo) plans to move back home to Mexico. She wants to become an American citizen but always gets nervous and crumbles during the oral exam.
159 23 "Vicki's Dilemma / Discount Romance / Loser & Still Champ" March 5, 1983
Vicki hooks up with a young passenger (Glenn Scarpelli) who gives her some pills he claims are supplements. After the boy gets ill, Doc (Bernie Kopell) deduces it's from drug use.
160 24 "So Help Me Hannah / The Maid Cleans Up / C.P.R., I.O.U." March 12, 1983
A woman(Mary Martin)'s ability to dispense advice irritates the Captain as well as the father (Max Showalter) of a young man who wants to pursue his own path (Timothy Patrick Murphy).
161 25 "Going to the Dogs / Putting on the Dog / Women's Best Friend / Whose Dog Is It Anyway" March 26, 1983
A contest is held on board to determine which dog will be on the cover of dog food containers: A woman (Catherine Bach) thinks a dog belonging to another passenger (Dirk Benedict) is one she lost some time ago.
162 26 "The Professor Has Class / When the Magic Disappears / We, the Jury" April 2, 1983
Professor Roscoe Weber (Sam Jaffe), a 91-year-old Nobel Prize winner for psychology, doesn't realize that his adoring former pupil, Professor Helen Burton (Bettye Ackerman), has been hired by his university to replace him.
163 27 "Hits and Missus / Return of Annabelle / Just Plain Folks Medicine / Caught in the Act / The Real Thing / Do Not Disturb / Lulu & Kenny (Country Music Jamboree) – Part 1" April 30, 1983
Aboard a country music jamboree cruise: Gopher thwarts an incognito Dottie West's plans for a restful weekend. He also borrows kitchen implements without asking from Chef Loomis (Pat Buttram) so the crew can create an opening act for the jamboree.
164 28 "Hits and Missus / Return of Annabelle / Just Plain Folks Medicine / Caught in the Act / The Real Thing / Do Not Disturb / Lulu & Kenny (Country Music Jamboree) – Part 2" April 30, 1983
Ted gives Annabelle an ultimatum: either the kids fly home or he does. Effie gets sick, but refuses to let Doc examine her.
165 29 "The Fountain of Youth / Bad Luck Cabin / Uncle Daddy" May 7, 1983
Decades ago, Dwight Scoffield (David Wayne) bilked Henry Harper in a land deal. Taking a cruise with granddaughter Stacy (Michelle Tobin), Dwight encounters Henry (Ted McGinley), who claims a fountain of youth is responsible for his apparently not aging in the past fifty years.
