Our Gang filmography


Section Title: The Roach/MGM talkies (1929–1938, The Little Rascals)
Table Section Text: These sound Our Gang shorts were produced by Hal Roach Studios and distributed to theaters by Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer. These 79 films, excepting some that have been removed for content, are the Our Gang shorts that King World Productions have packaged and syndicated as The Little Rascals.
