List of deaths due to injuries sustained in boxing


Section Title:
Table Section Text: The following is a list of deaths due to injuries sustained in boxing. In February 1995 it was estimated that "approximately 500 boxers have died in the ring or as a result of boxing since the Marquess of Queensberry Rules were introduced in 1884." 22 boxers died in 1953 alone.
