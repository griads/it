ISO/IEC 7810


Section Title: Card sizes
Table Section Text: The standard defines four card sizes: ID-1, ID-2, ID-3 and ID-000. All card sizes have a thickness of 0.76 mm (1/32 in).
Format Dimensions Usage
ID-1 85.60 × 53.98 mm Most banking cards and ID cards
ID-2 105 × 74 mm French and other ID cards; Visas
ID-3 125 × 88 mm US government ID cards
ID-000 25 × 15 mm SIM cards
