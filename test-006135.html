September 22, 2014 Tucker Hipps Sigma Phi Epsilon Clemson University Head injuries after fall Sigma Phi Epsilon pledge Tucker Hipps was participating in a group run with his fraternity around 5:30am when he fell from a bridge into shallow waters of Lake Hartwell.
