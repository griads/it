134 16 "Blast the Past" Daniel Attias P.K. Simonds & Melissa Rosenberg March 14, 2000

135 17 "Getting There" Joe Penella Tom Garrigus March 21, 2000
Unable to find his calling, Bailey becomes reacquainted with Holly, where he attempts to help her older sister, Fiona, find her calling as a costume designer.
136 18 "Too Cool for School" Scott Brazil Ian Biederman & Mimi Schmir April 4, 2000

137 19 "Isn't It Romantic" Ken Topolsky Allan Heinberg April 11, 2000
Just as Bailey begins taking business college classes, Holly's student visa is revoked, which means that she must return to England.
138 20 "Great Expectations" Christian D. Della Penna Emily Whitesell April 18, 2000
Will panics after a meeting with an INS agent asking about the authenticity of his and Holly's "marriage," while Bailey's discomfort grows after Will and Holly pretend to act intimate in public.
139 21 "Taboo or Not Taboo" Matthew Fox Mimi Schmir & Melissa Rosenberg April 19, 2000
Claudia is tempted by an academic shortcut of plagiarizing a term paper while trying to balance her time between school and seeing Todd.
140 22 "Falling Forward" David Dworetzky Allan Heinberg & Tom Garrigus & Ian Biederman April 26, 2000

141 23 "All's Well..." Steve Robman P.K. Simonds May 3, 2000
Bailey considers moving to Philadelphia to go back to college following Joe's advice, but has some complications when Owen does not want to go with him and Charlie tells Bailey about keeping their parental responsibilities.
142 24 "...That Ends Well" Ken Topolsky Christopher Keyser & Amy Lippman May 3, 2000
In the series finale, Bailey, Julia and Claudia all announce their plans to leave town for their own individual careers during the weekly family dinner at Salinger's Restaurant.
