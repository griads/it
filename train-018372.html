2001, July 8 Kirstin "Blaise" Lobato Murder of Duran Bailey Las Vegas, Nevada 40 to 100 years in prison 16 years Yes
In May 2001, Lobato, then 18, was staying at a residential motel in Las Vegas when she was allegedly attacked by a black man who "smelled like alcohol and dirty diapers." She was able to escape after she pulled out a knife and slashed the man's groin.

Ferguson, who was 17 at the time of the murder, was convicted on the basis of a friend's confession to police claiming he and Ferguson killed Heitholt for drinking money.
